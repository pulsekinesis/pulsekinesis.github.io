Game Development History

I started out as a Roblox game developer in 2013, doing odd projects while I was studying. I was completely self taught, and I learned most of my programming skills through asking various questions to much more skilled developers & reading through documentation.

My first "huge" game project was "The Undead Coming" in 2021. While in hindsight I could've done better with the game, it taught me a lot about game development. I eventually ended up connecting with Fezezen, and we began working on "Guts & Blackpowder" together (which became my 2nd biggest game project).

Currently, I'm working on other projects both within and outside of Roblox.
